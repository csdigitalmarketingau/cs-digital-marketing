# Google Ranking Service - Terms

**Between:** CS Digital Marketing (ABN [your ABN]) and [Client business name] ("you").

**1. What you get**
A 12-week programme to get your business showing up at the top of Google when local people
search for what you do. That includes:
- Setting up and improving your Google business listing.
- Tidying up your website so Google trusts it.
- Listing your business on 15+ trusted online directories, with the exact same details
  everywhere.
- Getting you set up to collect more Google reviews, and replying to them.
- A short update each week on what we have done, and a monthly report showing where you rank.

**2. Price and payment**
- $500 a month, paid at the start of each month. We start once your first payment is in and
  you have given us access to your Google listing.
- The programme runs for 12 weeks. After that we keep it going month to month, and you can
  stop anytime before the next month.

**3. The 90-day guarantee**
We are confident enough to put it in writing. Within 90 days of starting, we will get your
business into the top 3 of the map results on Google for one agreed search, for example
"marriage celebrant Mandurah".
We agree that search with you in writing at the start, and it has to be realistic for your
area. Very large, big-city searches are handled separately.

We check your ranking in a fair way: a normal Google search with no account logged in, done
from your main suburb. We do not count searches you do while logged in or while sitting at your
business, because Google bumps you up in those. We check the same way at the start and again at
day 90, so the before and after match.

If we do not get you there by day 90, and you have done your part (section 4), you pick one: a
full refund of everything you have paid, or we keep working for free until you get there. Your
choice.

**4. What we need from you**
The guarantee depends on these:
- You make us a manager on your Google listing (you never have to give us your password).
- You give us your business details, services, the areas you cover, photos, and quick replies
  when we ask.
- You do not make your own changes to the listing during the programme without telling us.
- You ask your customers for reviews when we prompt you (we give you the link and the words).
- Your Google listing is a real, verified business that Google has not banned.
If these are not met, the guarantee pauses or does not apply for that time.

**5. Timing and delays**
The 90 days start when your payment is in and we have access to your Google listing. If we are
waiting on you for access, info, photos, or feedback, the clock pauses for that time. We will
tell you when it is paused and why.

**6. What the guarantee does not cover**
- One business, one location, one agreed search per programme.
- We do not control Google. The guarantee covers the ranking we measure. It does not cover
  Google changing how it works, your listing being banned for something we did not do, or you
  making changes that undo our work.
- Brand-new businesses with no proper listing yet, or very competitive areas, are quoted
  separately and may not be covered by the guarantee.

**7. After the 12 weeks**
It rolls into month-to-month upkeep: keeping your listing active, posting, watching your
ranking, and handling reviews. Stop anytime before the next month. The guarantee covers the
first 12 weeks, not the ongoing upkeep.

**8. It is all yours**
Your Google listing, website, and directory listings belong to you. We work through manager
access and hand everything back clean whenever you ask. No lock-in.

**9. If something goes wrong**
We do the work with proper care. We are not responsible for things outside our control (Google
outages, bans we did not cause, or other websites changing). The most we can owe you is what
you have paid us.
