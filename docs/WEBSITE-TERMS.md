# Website Build — Terms of Service

Plain-English terms for a website build. Send with the invoice / in the Welcome Pack and
have the client reply "agreed" (a confirming email is enough to be binding). Replace
[brackets] per client. Keep a copy with the client's record.

---

**Between:** CS Digital Marketing (ABN [your ABN]) and [Client business name] ("you").

**1. What you get**
A custom website: home, services, service-area pages, about, and contact, built mobile-first
and fast, with on-page SEO set up (titles, headings, schema, sitemap) and a contact/quote
form that emails you enquiries. Includes the first 12 months of hosting.

**2. Price and payment**
- Build: $750, one-off. 50% ($375) deposit to start, 50% ($375) on launch.
- Work begins once the deposit and your content are received.
- From 12 months after launch: $300/year for hosting, domain renewal, security, uptime,
  and minor edits. Invoiced at renewal. You can cancel before any renewal.

**3. Minor edits (what the $300/year covers)**
Small text and photo changes, swapping details, adding the odd service. As a guide, up to
about one hour of changes a year. Larger work (new page sections, redesigns, extra pages)
is quoted separately before any work starts.

**4. Timeline**
Roughly two weeks from when we have your deposit and content. Delays in getting content or
feedback from you push the timeline out.

**5. What we need from you**
Your business details, services, service areas, logo (or we create a simple text mark),
photos, and timely feedback. See the Welcome Pack for the full list.

**6. Revisions**
Two rounds of revisions are included during the build. Further changes after sign-off fall
under minor edits or are quoted.

**7. Domain**
As part of the build we register a domain on your behalf from your preference list. The
domain is yours. If you ever leave, we transfer it to you.

**8. Ownership and no lock-in**
There is no lock-in contract. You can ask us to hand over your website files and domain at
any time and we will, within a reasonable period. Hosting and the domain are ongoing costs;
if you stop the yearly fee, the site will need hosting elsewhere to stay online.

**9. Cancellation**
If you cancel before launch, the deposit covers work done to that point and is not refunded.
Any balance for work not yet done is not charged.

**10. Liability**
We build and maintain the site with reasonable care. We are not liable for losses outside
our control (third-party outages, etc.). Our total liability is limited to the fees you have
paid us.

By paying the deposit or replying "agreed", you accept these terms.
